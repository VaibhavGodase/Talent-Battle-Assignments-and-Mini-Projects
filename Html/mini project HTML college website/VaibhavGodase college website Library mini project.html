Library

Central library was started in 1983. The main objective of central library is to provide seamless access to information to its users in a networked environment and to become a premier Learning Resource Centre in Engineering, Science & Technology and related areas. The library is fully automated with Sack Info 2.5 ERP library management system (LMS) with barcode technology. The library has rich collection of text book, reference books, handbooks on Mechanical Engineering, Production Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Electronic Engineering, E & TC Engineering, Engineering Science, Electrical Engineering and MBA The total number of books available in library is 66798 volumes and 14926 titles as on 21stApril, 2022 and also library has subscribed 109 Print Journals,10 Magazine &270 E-Journals. The library has covering an area of 1222.00 square meters of main branch & 1368 suare meter with sub branches. It has well designed and maintained stack rooms. The library database is centrally computerized for accession and renewals with smart card for the students and bar codes for the books for easy, secure and quick operation. Student can search the books through Web-OPAC.
