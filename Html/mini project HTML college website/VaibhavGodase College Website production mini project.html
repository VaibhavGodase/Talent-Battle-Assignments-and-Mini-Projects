Production Department

The department is well known in Pune University for its rank holders. As per ‘US Market Research Analyst’ survey, Production Engineering branch has bright future in new era. Highly qualified & senior faculties are involved in R & D of conventional/ Non-conventional machining & industrial engineering areas.

Department has following laboratories-

Robotics lab (e- yantra, in association with IIT Powai)

Metrology & Quality Control Lab

Metal Cutting & Tool Design Lab

Metallurgy Lab

Material forming lab

Advanced Machine Shop

Project Lab.

CAD/ CAM Lab.

Welding & foundry Lab.

Digital CAM lab.

The CAD/ CAM Lab is equipped with latest softwares like Unigraphics, MasterCAM, Pro-E, CatiaV5, Ansys, Solid Edge, Witness. The department runs certificate courses on these software for the students. Department regularly organizes National level workshop/ seminars, paper presentations etc. Students participate in various extra & co-curricular activities through Production Engineering Students’ Association (PESA). Department started AeSI Students chapter in association with Aeronautical Society of India, Nasik Branch.
