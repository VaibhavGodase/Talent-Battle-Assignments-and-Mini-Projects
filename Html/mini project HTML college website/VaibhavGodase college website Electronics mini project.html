Elecronics Department

Department of Electronics is an interdisciplinary course started in 1986 with the intake of 60. The department is accredited two times by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi. The department has well qualified and experienced staff to cater the University curriculum. All Laboratories are well equipped with latest software and hardware. Department has 101 computers with latest configuration and necessary peripherals to fulfil the students requirements. The available licensed software are Xillinx, NI Lab View, Matlab, ALTIUM Designer, Mentor Graphics EDA tools and Multisim. Various workshops /seminars/value addition programs in collaboration with industries such as FOX Solutions India Pvt. Ltd, OS3 Technology on VLSI Design, Lab View programming, Internet of Things, Matlab programming, PLC and SCADA, Variable Frequency Drives, Robotics are organized regularly for the students.

The department has Electronics& Computer Engineering Student Association (EESA) under which different events like GD, Quiz, Circuit Design contest, debate, project exhibition, indoor and outdoor games are organized. Hobby club is established to make the student practically sound and develop their interest by implementing electronic Gadgets for variety of applications. Guest lectures/Seminars on Advancement in Technology, Entrepreneurship development, Competitive Examinations (GATE/GRE/TOFEL), Social awareness/responsibilities, are arranged for the students. The department organizes various Technical events on State/National levels..
